Alameda Municipal Power: Building a Modern Digital Experience for Customer Programs

Client: Alameda Municipal Power (AMP)
Industry: Public Utility — Energy Services
Solutions: Salesforce Experience Cloud — Customer Program Portal; AI-Enabled DevOps Pipeline
Implementation Partner: SuccessMetrics

The Challenge

Alameda Municipal Power manages a diverse portfolio of residential and commercial customer programs, each with unique eligibility criteria, documentation requirements, and approval workflows. Manual and fragmented processes created growing operational strain: application processing was slow, customers had no visibility into status, document handling was largely manual, and leadership lacked real-time insight into program performance.

Solution

SuccessMetrics implemented a Salesforce Experience Cloud platform that replaced fragmented manual processes with a modern, integrated digital experience. At the center is a secure customer self-service portal that lets residential and commercial customers submit applications online, upload supporting documents, complete digital signatures electronically, and track application status in real time. More than 25 customer program applications were digitized, creating a consistent experience across AMP's full service offering.

Behind the portal, intelligent process automation handles operational complexity: applications are routed and assigned automatically, multi-level review and approval workflows enforce program-specific rules, and customers receive automated notifications at each stage. Queue-based work management gives AMP staff clear visibility into what needs attention, while real-time dashboards and reporting provide leadership with actionable insight into application volumes, processing timelines, and program performance.

Originally planned for an 18-week implementation, compliance-driven requirements demanded a go-live within 12 weeks. To meet this accelerated timeline while reducing risk, SuccessMetrics embedded an AI-enabled DevOps pipeline. AI-assisted code generation and automated review cycles cut build time significantly, automated regression testing reduced QA effort by 30%, and security checks were shifted left into the pipeline—ensuring DevSecOps compliance and accelerating overall delivery by approximately 30%.

The Impact

  • Digitized 25+ programs — residential and commercial applications now managed end-to-end on a single platform
  • Improved customer experience — fully self-service portal with real-time status tracking replaces manual, opaque processes
  • Reduced manual effort — automated routing, approvals, and notifications eliminate repetitive administrative work
  • Accelerated review cycles — applications move through approval workflows faster with fewer hand-offs and delays
  • 40% faster development — AI-assisted code generation and automated review cycles cut build time significantly
  • 30% less QA effort — automated regression testing freed the team to focus on complex integration scenarios
  • Enhanced operational visibility — dashboards surface program performance metrics that were previously unavailable
  • Scalable foundation — a modernized platform and pipeline built to support future program expansion and faster iteration

AMP now has a future-ready engagement platform and a development pipeline that can keep pace with it—strengthening service delivery, reducing operational risk, and supporting AMP's commitment to serving the Alameda community more effectively.
